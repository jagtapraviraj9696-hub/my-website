📖 About Project

EcoSmartVillage is a Community Engagement Project that promotes water conservation, waste management, organic farming, and renewable energy.

🎯 Project Objectives

  • Promote water conservation in villages.
  • Encourage proper waste management.
  • Support organic farming practices.
  • Promote renewable energy usage.
  • Create awareness about environmental protection.

🌟 Benefits

  • Clean and healthy environment.
  • Reduced water wastage.
  • Improved soil fertility.
  • Reduced pollution.
  • Sustainable village development.

💧 Water Conservation

Save water by fixing leaks, use rainwater harvesting and avoid water wastage.

♻️ Waste Management

Separate wet and dry waste. Recycle plastic, paper and metal.

🌱 Organic Farming

Use organic fertilizers and reduce chemical pesticides for healthy crops.

☀️ Renewable Energy

Use solar and wind energy to reduce pollution and save electricity.
