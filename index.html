🌍 Welcome to EcoSmartVillage

EcoSmartVillage is a Community Engagement Project developed to promote environmental awareness, water conservation, waste management, organic farming and renewable energy.

📖 About Project

EcoSmartVillage is a modern village development website created to spread awareness about clean environment, water conservation, organic farming, renewable energy and sustainable village development.

🎯 Project Objectives

  • Promote water conservation.
  • Reduce pollution.
  • Encourage organic farming.
  • Promote renewable energy.
  • Create environmental awareness.

🌟 Benefits

  • Clean village.
  • Healthy environment.
  • Save water.
  • Improve farming.
  • Sustainable development.

💧 Water Conservation

Save water by fixing leaks, using rainwater harvesting, and avoiding unnecessary wastage.

♻️ Waste Management

Separate wet and dry waste. Recycle plastic, paper and metal to keep the village clean.

🌱 Organic Farming

Use organic fertilizers and natural farming techniques to improve soil fertility.

☀️ Renewable Energy

Use solar and wind energy to reduce pollution and save electricity.
